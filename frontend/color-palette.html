Key Design Principles

  • Accent stays vibrant: The electric gold #F5D547 is the SAME in both modes ‚Äî never brown or muted
  • Text contrast: Dark text (#1a1d23) on light backgrounds, light text (#e6e9ef) on dark
  • Muted is readable: #6b7280 (light) and #a6b0bf (dark) provide sufficient contrast
  • Sparklines pop: Use the accent gold against both backgrounds ‚Äî visible everywhere
  • Buttons: Primary = solid gold with dark text. Secondary = ghost style with border
  • Green tint adjusted: Slightly deeper green (#16A34A) for light mode readability
